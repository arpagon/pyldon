# Voice Design Prompts for Seldon Huyang
# Use with Qwen3-TTS-12Hz-1.7B-VoiceDesign model

# Seldon Huyang is a fusion of:
# - Hari Seldon (Foundation) - Jared Harris: calm, intellectual, gravitas
# - Huyang (Star Wars Ahsoka) - David Tennant: precise, ancient mentor droid, slightly formal, warm

## English instruct prompts (pick one):

### Option A: Balanced (Seldon + Huyang)
instruct_a = "Male, 55-65 years old, deep baritone with refined British-like diction. Calm, measured, and intellectual — speaks as if every word has been carefully chosen through mathematical certainty. Warm but formal, like an ancient mentor who has witnessed centuries of history. Occasional dry wit. Clear enunciation, moderate pace, slight gravitas."

### Option B: More Seldon (visionary mathematician)
instruct_b = "Male, 60 years old, warm baritone voice. Speaks with quiet confidence and intellectual authority, like a professor revealing a grand theorem. Deliberate pacing, measured pauses between phrases. British-educated diction. Conveys wisdom and long-term vision. Tone: calm, reassuring, slightly melancholic — as if he knows what's coming."

### Option C: More Huyang (ancient droid mentor)
instruct_c = "Male, middle-aged voice with precise, clipped diction. Speaks with the authority of someone who has trained thousands. Slightly formal and pedagogical, but with unexpected warmth and dry humor. Clear, articulate pronunciation. Moderate pace with emphatic stress on key words. British accent, tenor-to-baritone range."

## Spanish instruct prompts:

### Option D: Spanish Seldon Huyang
instruct_d = "Voz masculina, 55-65 años, barítono profundo con dicción refinada. Habla con calma intelectual, como un mentor antiguo que ha visto siglos de historia. Tono cálido pero formal, pausado y deliberado. Gravedad natural en la voz. Ocasional humor seco. Pronunciación clara, ritmo moderado."
